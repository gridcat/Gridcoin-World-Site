Pros and Cons of Account Managers

  • Choose and follow the instructions for your choice
  • Use an Account Manager:
    • Single Sign On: One password for BOINC to remember.
    • Manage computer resources & configuration from one location.
    • Manage all BOINC projects from one management portal online.
  • Disadvantages:
    • Single Sign On: If your account manager's credentials are compromised, then all of your BOINC project accounts will be compromised.

No Account Manager Setup

  • After starting BOINC, select the "add a project" button.
  • After clicking "Next" choose a project.
  • For some projects (such as World Community Grid) you have to register directly on the homepage of the project. Remember to use the same email address for all BOINC projects.
  • Confirm the registration. After tapping the "Finish" button, a browser should open. If not, go to the project homepage (can be found via Google). Find the “Your Account” button and press it.
  • Within the web page, enter your username (country/post-code can be blank) then press OK.
  • Search for and join team "Gridcoin".
  • Repeat these steps for all projects you want to participate in.

BOINC Account Manager Setup

  • Create a BAM! account here.
  • Wait for the activation mail, and activate your account.
  • Login to BAM! (the login box is at the top-right of this page).
  • Go to "Sign-up for projects" in the BAM! menu and click the "Create account" icon for every project you want to join.
  • Joining team Gridcoin:
    • Go to the Team page.
    • For each project, tick the checkbox in the 3rd column.
    • Click the green "Change/Join team" button at the bottom of the list of projects.
    • Enter "Gridcoin" in the popup text field & click the "Search" button.
    • Select team "Gridcoin" in each dropdown menu & click the green "Change/join team" button.
    • Click the orange "back to overview" button.
  • Go to "My projects" in the BAM! menu and select the projects that you want all your new hosts to attach to by clicking the "Attach new host by default" icon.
  • Attach your BOINC client to BAM!. The instructions are here.
  • The client will make two connections to BAM!. It will connect to projects that are set to default attach.
  • Configure BAM! to your liking. Read the F.A.Q. for details.
